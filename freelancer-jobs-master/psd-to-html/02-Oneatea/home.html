‘One’atea – relaxing holiday accommodation on Hunga Island, Vava’u, in the beautiful kingdom of Tonga

Talitali fiefia – welcome

Welcome to ‘One’atea. Our comfortable Pacific holiday home situated on ‘One’atea beach inside the stunning Hunga Lagoon. The perfect place to relax and enjoy one of Vava’u’s most beautiful settings.

Just a few steps down from the deck, the crystal clear, blue water welcomes you. A safe, gentle swimming haven that is warm most of the year; turtles can be seen bobbing cheekily in the water amongst schools of fish in front of our home. Whales are often seen just outside the Lagoon.

Explore the sheltered lagoon by kayak or boat and venture out to visit neighbouring islands. Fish off the beach or take the boat out to catch a variety of fish for your dinner. Visit the local village or try local foods and crafts.
