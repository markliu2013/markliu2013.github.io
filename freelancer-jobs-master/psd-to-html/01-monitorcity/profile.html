Our Company

Mission statement

Monitor City was founded to provide packaged audio systems for concert touring, festivals and events using the most advanced audio technology available with networking and control systems that are second to none. We aim to provide bespoke systems using best practice in sound reinforcement audio systems and to ensure clients, staff, production managers and sound engineers can achieve results of the highest standard in audio production. Our company systems and procedures ensure that all events we supply to are successful for all involved

Goals

  • Innovate and Develop custom solutions that are the best available
  • Never compromise on where it counts in an audio signal path
  • Package systems for preservation and low maintenance when on tour
  • Always have happy clients, sound engineers and performers

industry experience & history

Audio Engineering Best Practice

Monitor City was established in August 2006 by Adrian Barnard and Matt Dufty; both Audio Engineers with a passion for using the highest quality systems for the best results. Our mission has been to supply Audio Systems with the best in custom designed packaging and cabling and to seek out products that allow us to do this with constant improvement

The systems that we have for production are based on many of our experiences over the length of our careers in the audio industry. We are determined to do things properly and pay attention to detail. Both directors have years of experience achieving high standards at the shows that matter, on time, and with excellent results. Touring internationally and nationally, the company founders have seen the way it is done on the most detailed productions worldwide and have dealt with many production companies. This has lead to a detailed knowledge of the market and the needs of the most demanding of artists & uncompromising situations. Through our International associations we have also been able to develop a market for supply to visiting international artists and supply systems to a standard that they are accustomed to

All clients of Monitor City can expect this same experience to mean a high standard for their job, and an attention to detail

Production Management & Logistics

Adding to this audio production background is many years in tour and production management leading to a superior network of suppliers to complete the picture for any event. Monitor City has been a capable principle or main contractor for concerts and event production. Using our targeted relationships, we have supplied financial, logistical, technical and personnel administration for a variety of entertainment events

Our goal has always been to develop long term, mutually beneficial partnerships with clients that require our core product and services - we do audio systems in an excellent fashion and subcontract the best people we know to provide a complete solution for the client be it for staging or lighting or freight management. As individuals we have been behind some of the best artists and festivals in the market in the many years prior to the forming of Monitor City, adding a supply element to an already wealth of experience and contacts within the field of audio production and more broadly in production of events and concerts

We have also developed strong relationships with other production companies in the australian market over the years which has made us able to meet any capacity when required
