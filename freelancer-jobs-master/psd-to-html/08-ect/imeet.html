iMeet is your own personal video conferencing meeting room

iMeet is a video conferencing software solution accessible from Australia and across the globe, from any desktop, laptop or smartphone*. If you need to sell, share, train or just need a quick brainstorm, now you can in your own personal video conferencing room.

Key Features

  • Face-to-face meetings

    See and meet face-to-face with webcam, quality video and screen sharing capabilities

  • Hassle-free access

    no downloads required for your guest access

  • Productivity tools

    iMeet for Desktop provides access to iMeet’s Toolbar for Outlook, room key and screen share

  • Meet anywhere, any time

    Host and join meetings with iMeet HD for iPad and iMeet for iPhone*

  • Keep it Private

    Assigned key access to your room to monitor who’s in your room

  • Share content

    Share files through your secure content library in your room

  • Choose your audio connection

    Call your computer, call your phone/mobile or dial in from any phone

  • Personalise your experience

    With your room name and personal URL. Create a personal bio, connect your social media links and privacy settings to personalise your meeting experience

  • Record, stop and rewind

    record sessions for on-demand video viewing

Benefits by Role

HR and recruitment professionals

iMeet can benefit HR and recruitment professionals:

  • Increase your reach and make a more personal connection with dispersed candidates
  • Faster hiring cycles by narrowing the field of talent with video conferencing
  • Use as a key tool in breaking down barriers in communication between employees, management, and HR representatives

Sales professionals

iMeet can benefit Sales professionals:

  • Providing prospects or customers with an option to meet face-to-face online
  • Sales teams can connect with customers through social media integration
  • Connecting remote sales teams for regular pipeline reviews and result briefings

Executive Assistants

iMeet can benefit Executive Assistants:

  • Manage schedules and coordinate meetings instantly via your desktop, laptop or smartphone*
  • Organise time critical meetings quickly within a face to face environment
  • Save time and distribute presentations and agendas within the meeting with easy file download
  • Conduct critical business conversations with privacy in Executive private iMeet rooms
  • Record meetings and review on-demand for minute and note taking
