An on-demand and reserved teleconferencing service to meet, work and collaborate.

ECT offers MeetNow On-demand; one of the easiest and most cost effective teleconferencing services. MeetNow On-demand is available 24/7 to discuss, share or conduct a quick brainstorm.

  • Conference call any time, no need to book or require any special equipment
  • MeetNow is secure with easy to use features for participant roll call, entry and exit announcements
  • MeetNow with up to 125 participants at any time
  • Over 100 local and international access numbers for all your global counterparts

ECT also offers MeetNow Reserved teleconferencing service. This conference call is a one off ctonference and needs to be booked each time you require a meeting.
