OpAssist is a fully managed teleconferencing webinar service to present, share and collaborate

OpAssist is ECT’s fully managed teleconferencing webinar service. OpAssist provides the attention to detail and quality of care you need during important meetings, interviews and events.

  • Fully trained event managers providing a professional touch for high-profile events
  • Welcome your guests with a personalised service connecting guests into the webinar
  • Create a formal atmosphere with music on entry to the webinar
  • Capture information from guests at entry for marketing research
  • Record your webinar for on-demand reviewing and archiving
