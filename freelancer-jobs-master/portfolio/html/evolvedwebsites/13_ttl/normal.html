Chicken

By all measures, Tablebirds is PNG's largest producer and distributor of fresh and frozen chicken. Strategically, we manufacture and supply stockfeed, fresh eggs, and day old chicks.

Quality chicken meat is our core business. Long established in Lae, Morobe Province, we have large breeder farms, hatcheries and a processing plant, all within the Markham Valley.

Size matters. Due to the number of farms we own and operate, coupled with the number of local growers that we engage, Tablebirds is able to increase or taper production in response to market requirements, an essential factor in ensuring freshness of product, and continuity of supply.

We supply chicken to manufacturers, wholesalers, retailers, catering companies, hotels and restaurants, on a daily basis as required.
