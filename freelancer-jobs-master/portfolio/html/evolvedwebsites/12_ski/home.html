Welcome to Selkie Designs

Here at Selkie Designs, we love to promote natural, beautiful top quality products that Nurture your child and don't cost the earth!

Give your child the very best, with a Selkiedesigns Amber Teething Necklace or Bracelet, or find matching Amber Jewellery for mum and dad.

Now with 21 different colours designs to choose from, there is something for everyone.

Selkiedesigns Amber Teething Jewellery is made with love and care with the comfort and wellbeing of your child for-most in our thoughts!

All Selkiedesigns products are made right here in Australia out of the finest quality materials available.
