DESCRIPTION

Baltic Amber Teething Necklace.

Bead Size: Approx 6.5 -7.5mm
Bead Shape: Smooth Polished Rounded Beads.
Weight: Approx 8 Grams.
Available In 2 Lengths: 33-34cm and 36-37cm.

(All measurements are approximate).

Please take the time to measure the neck of your child before choosing a necklace length. An easy way to do this is to take a piece of string and drape it around the neck of your child to where you would like it to sit, and then measure that against a ruler.

Ideally, your child should not be able to get the necklace in his/her mouth.I am able to make up custom lengths upon request.

Directions For Use

  1. Place necklace, bracelet or anklet on the child against their skin, as the jewellery is for wearing only.
  2. Do NOT let the child put the jewellery in their mouth, suck, chew or play with the jewellery.
  3. Supervise the child at all times when they are wearing the jewellery.
    Remove jewellery from the child when they are sleeping and when the child is unattended or unsupervised.
