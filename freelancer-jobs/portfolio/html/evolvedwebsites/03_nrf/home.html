About Northern Rivers Food

Northern Rivers Food is a proactive group established for the benefit of all food businesses that grow, manufacture, distribute, sell or serve food in the Northern Rivers region of New South Wales. We provide members with appropriate growth ingredients:

  • reliable market data
  • up to date information,
  • resource register,
  • networking and mentoring forums,
  • targeted marketing opportunities
