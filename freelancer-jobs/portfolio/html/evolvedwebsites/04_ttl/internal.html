Green Tea Brewing Tips

This tea is brewed simply by infusing one teaspoon per cup in a tea pot or plunger, with boiling water. Let steep for 2 minutes to ensure the best flavour - strain and serve.

It is recommended that Green Tea Tonic is enjoyed freshly made and not left to steep too long (only 2 minutes) as it can develop a bitter taste if over steeped.
