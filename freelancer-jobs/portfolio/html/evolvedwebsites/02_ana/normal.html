Anna Middleton Boutique

After many interesting and successful years of manufacturing, I now have a wonderful shop in Byron Bay, a top priority for a holiday destination.
To walk in and to walk out feeling absolutely fabulous is the service we provide.
For many years I have had so much fun sourcing high end labels and internationally revered designs from around the globe and lots of exciting Australian labels for lower budgets, enabling the store to cater to everyone. Interesting bags, belts, jewelry and shoes to compliment an outfit are also tantalizing when you shop with us.

We delight in sending parcels Australia wide so don't hesitate to contact us as we are only too happy to please.
