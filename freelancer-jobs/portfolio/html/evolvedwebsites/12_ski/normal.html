Why Choose Selkie Designs

There are a few reasons why Selkiedesigns Teething necklaces are bettern than most.

  • Selkiedesigns necklaces are strung on a thick gauge silk for Natural High Tensile Strength. Silk is a non-toxic natural fibre.
  • Selkiedesigns necklaces are Non-Toxic, being fastened with a good quality 925 sterling silver clasp, not a plastic screw clasp which uses glue to fasten the thread in one end. Selkiedesigns uses a natural silk thread, not synthetic or cotton/synthetic blend.
  • There is also a large sterling silver ring (as part of the clasp) on one end of the necklace, which is designed to open at the split if the necklace is pulled firmly. The large ring will open just enough to release the small ring on the same end of the necklace. Therefore if your child becomes caught on anything whilst wearing the necklace, the ring will open and the necklace will fall off, releasing your child without breaking the necklace. If the clasp or ring breaks or becomes lost, please discontinue use immediately and return the necklace to Selkiedesigns for repair* (see Guarantee information). Please email: heidi@selkiedesigns.com.au for repair. Please note: This feature is not 'Factory Tested' and your child's teething jewellery should be used in accordance with the safety instructions provided on this website.
  • Each bead in the necklace is individually knotted so that in the event that the necklace breaks (where excessive force is applied) only one bead will come off and the remaining beads will stay intact. Thus, a safety measure to assist with preventing an occurrence where all of the beads fall off the necklace and become a potential choking hazard to the child.
  • Selkiedesigns uses Real, Genuine Baltic Amber. Selkiedesigns does not use melted, pressed or reconstituted amber or amberoid. This means that each bead is cut from a larger piece of amber. No fused chips are used.
  • Selkiedesigns teething necklaces contain a heavier weight of amber than the majority of imported necklaces (Approx 8gm per 33-34cm necklace).
  • Selkiedesigns teething necklaces are pre-stretched, and strung on thick silk, which has an excellent tensile strength. So over the life of the necklace, it will not stretch more that a few centimeters. And with the appropriate care, will last longer.
  • Selkiedesigns teething necklaces are made with rounded beads for greater comfort and surface contact.
