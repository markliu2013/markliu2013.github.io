Activities

Swimming, snorkelling, fishing, kayaking and boating are all great, right on your doorstep, inside the lagoon, or you might like to try other activities with, and arranged by your village host.

Fishing

Trolling outside the lagoon, 1–4 people, 2 hours – T$50 per boat load

Snorkelling

Trip to Vakaeatu coral gardens, 1–4 people, 3 hours – $75 per boat load

Blue Lagoon area, 1–4 people, 2 hours – T$50 per boat load

Around the false entrance – T$10 per hour

Coconut skills

Demonstrations. Selection and preparation for food and drink (including preparation of fresh coconut milk), see your village hosts

whale watching

Watch the humpback whales or even swim with them. Whale watching with our neighbours Ika Lahi Lodge July – October.

Dining Out

Ika Lahi Fishing Lodge

www.tongafishing.com

Our neighbours, just a short walk down the beach. Restaurant and bar. Great for a special night. Open May to December.
Bookings essential, preferably the day before. VHF 26 or 71, phone 70611

Blue Lagoon Resort

www.bluelagoontonga.com

Located in the neighbouring lagoon 15 minutes by boat. Open for fine food, June to September.
Bookings essential.
