Facilities

Small, comfortable, furnished two bedroom home situated on half an acre of beachfront property inside the beautiful deep water lagoon formed by Hunga, Kalau and Fofoa Islands

  • Suits up to 4 people, two bedrooms, one double and two singles
  • Modern open plan lounge, dining and kitchen with gas oven
  • Basic bathroom facilities with cold water shower
  • Solar powered - 24v & 12v fridge/freezer, lights, stereo, VHF radio, 240v inverter for charging etc
  • Two kayaks
  • Basic fishing gear - boat rod, softbait/casting rod, hand lines
  • 3.8m aluminium pontoon boat with 15hp outboard
  • Safe swimming, snorkelling and boating right on your doorstep
  • Hang out on the beach or deck, watch the fish, and maybe see the turtles swim by

Your village host will meet you in Neiafu and escort you to ‘One’atea, contact you most days and arrange activities if required.
