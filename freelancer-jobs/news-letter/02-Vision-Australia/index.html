More Australians than ever are losing their sight to conditions like diabetes and macular degeneration. For these people, simple daily activities – like walking to the bus stop, or simply getting around the home – can become a massive challenge.

What if you could help restore confidence and independence to Australians who are blind or have low vision by taking on your own personal challenge?

“I went blind in six weeks… it changed everything.”

Heidi was 23 years old and on top of the world, running her own business and planning her wedding. Until she discovered she was losing her sight.

“I thought all my dreams for the future were over,” she said.

Then Vision Australia stepped in. Heidi’s Orientation and Mobility Specialist Tracy was able to tailor a program for Heidi that catered to her individual needs.

“We focused on Heidi’s personal goals like being able to get out of the house, catch a bus to town and go shopping or meet her friends for coffee.”

Support provided by Vision Australia gave Heidi the confidence and independence she needed to keep moving and live her life to the full.
