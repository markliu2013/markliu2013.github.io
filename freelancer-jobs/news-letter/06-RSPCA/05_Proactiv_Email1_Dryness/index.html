#1 Know Your Skin
Make some changes
 
If your skin feels sensitive it may need to adjust to Proactiv.
1. Try using the system once a day, every other day for a week.
2. In week two, use it once daily.
3. By week three, you should be ready to apply twice daily.
4. If you are still experiencing sensitivity, call our Skin Care Advisors on 1800 338 383 Monday to Friday 7am-7pm, or Saturday 8am-4pm EST, and ask about ways to personalise your routine.
Moisturise
